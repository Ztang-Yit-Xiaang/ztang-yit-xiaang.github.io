This work studies optimization strategies for automated storage and retrieval systems (AS/RS) using **mixed-integer linear programming (MILP)**.

The research focuses on improving storage efficiency and operational performance in automated warehouse environments by modeling system constraints and operational objectives within a mathematical optimization framework.

Key aspects of the work include:

- Mathematical modeling of AS/RS operations
- Mixed-integer linear programming optimization
- Simulation and evaluation of storage system efficiency
- Applications to intelligent logistics and warehouse automation
